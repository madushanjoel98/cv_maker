Privacy & Data Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Core Promise: We operate on a "Client-Side First" architecture. Your personal CV data is processed entirely within your browser and is not stored on our servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Free CV Maker. We prioritize your privacy and data security. This policy outlines how we handle your information. Unlike traditional web services, we have designed our system to maximize your control, meaning the sensitive personal information you enter into your CV (like phone numbers, addresses, and work history) remains on your device.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal CV data.

  • Local Browser Storage: We use your browser's localStorage to auto-save your progress so you don't lose your work if you accidentally close the tab. This data lives on your device, not our servers.
  • No Database: We do not maintain a database of user CVs. When you click "Save", you are downloading a JSON file to your own device. When you "Load", you are reading that file from your device.
  • Image Uploads: Profile pictures are processed locally in your browser using Canvas API and are not uploaded to any remote server.

3. Analytics

To improve our service, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4). This helps us understand how users interact with the website (e.g., which templates are popular, page load times).

  • What is collected: Anonymized usage data, device type, region (country/city level), and potential interactions.
  • What is NOT collected: The actual text content of your CV, your specific inputs, or personally identifiable information (PII).

Use of Google Analytics is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. AI Features & Third-Party Services

Our AI text enhancement features are powered by Google's Gemini API.

  • API Keys: If you choose to use AI features, you must provide your own API key. This key is stored securely in your browser's Local Storage (gemini_key) and is never sent to our servers.
  • Data Transmission: When you use the "AI Enhance" button, the specific text you selected is sent directly from your browser to Google's servers for processing.
  • Data Retention: We do not log or store your AI prompts or the generated responses. Please refer to Google's AI Terms of Service for how they handle data sent to their API.

5. Your Rights & Control

Since your data is stored locally:

  • Access & Portability: You can download your data anytime using the "Save Schema/JSON" option in the editor.
  • Erasure: You can wipe all your data from the application by simply clearing your browser's cache/cookies or by using browser developer tools to clear localStorage.
  • Opt-Out: You can use this application in "Incognito" or "Private" mode to ensure no local data persists after your session ends.
