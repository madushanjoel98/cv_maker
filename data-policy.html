Data Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. About This Website

This is a free CV making website designed to help users create and download resumes easily. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, track, or share any personal information of users. All data you enter (such as name, education, skills, and experience) is used only to generate your CV in your browser.

3. No Accounts or Logins

This website does not require you to create an account or log in. You can use all features without providing any identifying information.

4. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking scripts, or third-party trackers to monitor your activity on this website.

5. Data Storage

Any information you enter remains only on your device and is not sent to our servers. Once you close or refresh the page, your data is not retained by us.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties because we do not collect it in the first place.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this data policy, you may contact the website administrator through the contact details provided on the website.
